WHO WE ARE

Peace4puppies is a registered 501(c)3 non-profit organization that specializes in the rehabilitation and rehoming of rescue dogs. We work tirelessly to improve the lives of these animals, and are committed to finding them all loving homes. Our team provides veterinary care, behavioral training, and socialization so that each dog is ready for a forever home when they leave our facility. With your help, we can change the luck of rescue dogs everywhere!
